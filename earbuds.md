## A set of green earbuds that allow seamless audio listening wherever

***

I am an individual who desperately needs some sort of audio while walking around, or standing at work. Sometimes silence is deafening for me, and being able to focus on some sort of coherent audio source, such as music or a podcast, helps immensely to pass the time, making commutes or time passing seem to fly by five times as fast.
